Volunteer Training

Welcome to the volunteer training section. This wiki contains all the information you need to become a successful volunteer in our accounting and finance student association.

Our training program is designed to provide you with the necessary skills and knowledge to assist our members and contribute to our organization's success.

Before you begin your volunteer journey, please ensure you have:

  • Completed the online orientation
  • Read our code of conduct
  • Attended the initial training session
  • Understood our organization's mission and values

For more information about our organization, please visit our main website.
