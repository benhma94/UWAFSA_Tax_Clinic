Volunteer Training

Training Overview -

Welcome to the Tax Clinic volunteer training program. This comprehensive guide will help you understand your role and responsibilities as a volunteer.

Our training program is designed to ensure that all volunteers are well-prepared to assist clients with their tax-related needs while maintaining the highest standards of professionalism and accuracy.

Getting Started +

Before beginning your volunteer work, please complete the following steps:

  • Review our volunteer handbook
  • Complete the online orientation module
  • Attend the mandatory training session
  • Sign the volunteer agreement

Once you've completed these steps, you'll be ready to start assisting clients.

Training Modules +

Our training program consists of several modules that cover different aspects of tax assistance:

  • Introduction to Tax Law
  • Client Interaction Skills
  • Form Preparation and Filing
  • Privacy and Confidentiality
  • Advanced Tax Topics

Each module includes interactive elements and assessments to ensure understanding.

Resources +

Additional resources to support your volunteer work:

  • Training manuals and guides
  • Online tax databases
  • Reference materials and calculators
  • Video tutorials
  • FAQs and troubleshooting guides

All resources are available through our secure volunteer portal.
